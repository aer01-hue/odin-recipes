Lasagna

Description

Make this delicious, tedious free lasagna tonight!

This dish is easy to make in half the time.

You and your family will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef (or ground turkey) and drain.
  2. Stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  3. Combine cheeses, eggs and seasonings.
  4. Assemble the lasagna, alternating meat, cheese and noodles.
  5. Bake, covered for 45 min.
  6. Remove cover , and cook an additional 10 min.
