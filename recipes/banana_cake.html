Banana Cake

Description

Try this moist and easy to make banana cake for a great snack or desert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup smashed bananas
  • 1 1.5 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Grease and flour a square pan.
  3. Melt sour cream and butter, add eggs. Then mix all ingredients until light and fluffy.
  4. stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Pour batter into the pan.
  6. Bake for 45 min or until toothpick comes out clean from center.
