Hot Dog Bun Garlic Toast

Description

This garlic bread is quick and easy to make. A great addition to your Italian meals.

Ingredients

  • 4 hot dog buns split
  • 2 tablespoons margarine
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablspoons grated parmesan
  • 1 teaspoon crushed, dried basil
  • Parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper or foil.
  2. In a small bowl, stir the Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, and basil.
  3. Spread margarine onto opened buns inside up, then sprinkle Parmesan mixture on top. Place onto baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
